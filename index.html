A Mushroom Growing Kit is a simple, space-saving, and effortless way to cultivate fresh, organic mushrooms right inside your own home.
01

Choose the Perfect Spot

Select a cool, slightly dark location away from direct sunlight, but with adequate ventilation (e.g., a quiet corner in your kitchen or room).

02

Open the Kit

Carefully make a small, precise cut on the plastic bag at the designated area as specified in the instruction leaflet.

03

Spray Water

Mist the cut area with clean water using a spray bottle 2 to 3 times a day. Keep it moist, but avoid overwatering.

Multiple Harvests

A single growing kit can continuously yield multiple fresh flushes of mushrooms over a span of 3 to 3.5 months.

Diverse Varieties

Easily cultivate premium varieties like Bhutanese, American Oyster, Pink Oyster, Abalone, Milky, and Shiitake mushrooms.

Effortless Gardening

No soil preparation, potting mixes, or heavy fertilizers required—making it absolutely perfect for busy individuals.

100% Hygienic & Safe

Prepared under strict quality standards to ensure you grow 100% organic, chemical-free, and healthy mushrooms at home.
